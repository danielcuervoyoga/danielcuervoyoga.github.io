About me

Hi, I’m Daniel Cuervo, a passionate yoga teacher from Colombia. My yoga journey began with a spark of curiosity and quickly grew into a deep and lifelong passion. After completing my foundational Vinyasa training at Awakening Yoga Academy with Patrick Beach and Carling Harps, I embraced teaching as my path and have been dedicated to guiding others ever since.

I’ve since deepened my knowledge with an additional 100 hours in Hatha yoga, mobility, and body mechanics, earning my 300-hour certification. My teaching style is rooted in creative sequencing, combining strength, mindfulness, and mobility to create well-rounded classes. I believe yoga is for everyone, regardless of where they start. My classes are designed to meet students where they are, building strength and intuition while embracing each person’s unique limitations.

I’m passionate about creating an environment where everyone feels comfortable and supported, no matter their experience level. My goal is for you to leave each practice feeling more connected, balanced, present, and truly happy in your body.

Join me on the mat, and let’s explore the beautiful synergy of mind, body, and spirit together.

Offerings

I offer a variety of classes designed for all levels, focusing on building strength, improving mobility, and cultivating mindfulness. Whether you're new to yoga or an experienced practitioner, my classes are created to meet you where you are. Here's a look at what I offer:

Private Yoga Sessions

Get personalized attention and guidance with one-on-one sessions. Private sessions are ideal for those looking to focus on specific goals, deepen their practice, or work through injuries. Each session is tailored to your needs and designed to provide the tools to move with more ease, strength, and confidence.

Group Classes

I offer group classes that are inclusive and accessible for all levels. Each class is designed to challenge you, build strength, and encourage a deeper connection to your body and breath. Some of the group classes include:

Vinyasa Flow: A dynamic class linking breath with movement to create a flowing, mindful practice. We’ll move through creative sequences that build strength and flexibility while promoting a deep mind-body connection.

Hatha Yoga: A slower-paced class focused on breath control, alignment, and building strength. This class is perfect for anyone looking to improve their posture, mobility, and balance.

Mobility & Body Mechanics: A class designed to improve joint mobility, functional movement, and overall body mechanics. Great for those looking to move more freely and efficiently in their daily lives.

Workshops & Events

I offer special events tailored to individuals or corporate groups, providing a unique and enriching experience. These events range from team-building yoga sessions to mindfulness workshops, designed to foster connection, reduce stress, and promote well-being. Whether for a corporate retreat, community gathering, or private event, my special events are customized to meet the specific needs of the group, creating a memorable and impactful experience.

Online Classes

My online classes bring the same thoughtful sequencing and mindful approach you experience in person, directly to your space. Whether you prefer the flexibility of recorded sessions or the live interaction of scheduled classes, my online offerings are designed to help you deepen your practice. With a focus on strength, mobility, and mindfulness, these classes are accessible at your convenience, so you can practice anytime, anywhere.
